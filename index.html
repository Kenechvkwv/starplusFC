Who We Are

We are a purpose-only football community who believe there is a better way to recognise and nurture emerging talent.

Starplus is a sports management company with the ability to scout in any region of Nigeria.

We have numerous competent scouts operating around Nigeria, a team of professionals in analytics, and opportunities to receive expert analysis from qualified coaches operating throughout Nigeria.

We create comprehensive player reports that include all aspects of the game, as well as his strengths and shortcomings.

We only cooperate with the very best. exclusively associate with extraordinary skills. We choose one another and benefit from one another.

Keep an eye on your player at all times. and it's crucial to maintain control over every aspect of life: and not just transfers.
